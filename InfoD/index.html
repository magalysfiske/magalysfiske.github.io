Art-Artist Final Poster

36 x 24 matte poster

For this project I focused on analyzing my relationship
with my education and what are things related to my education
that are the most important to me. Conducted research such
as surveys of classmates, online databases and self-reflection
for personal parts of the project. Four sketches were done
before the final product. During group critiques I would ask,
for advice on things like font choice and color palettes.
Most people preffered the navy blue and white pallet but agreed
with me that it could be pushed more. Once I brought a
sketch of the more vibrant color pallet my group was more
enthusiastic about the project.
