I’m a Boston area based designer currently working on my BFA in Graphic Design at UMASS
Dartmouth College of Visual and Performing Arts. I’m also striving for a printmaking minor
with a Book Arts focus.I’ve been involved with design since high school in my vocational
shop where I studied business technology and web design. I have interests in typography and experimental processes. I believe going too far with a piece is essential to knowing when
to pull back and find a perfect solution. Typography can be a huge factor in this process
and I love working in this way. My main practice involves HTML ,CSS, and Adobe products but
I’m always willing to learn more. Outside of work I enjoy running, illustration,painting,
and book arts.
